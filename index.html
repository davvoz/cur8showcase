Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about getting started with cur8.fun

What makes cur8.fun different from traditional social platforms?

cur8.fun is built on blockchain technology that ensures content ownership remains with creators. Unlike traditional platforms that profit from your data and content, cur8.fun directly rewards creators with cryptocurrency tokens based on community appreciation. There's no mysterious algorithm determining who sees your content—visibility is organic and community-driven.

How do I earn rewards on cur8.fun?

When users upvote your content, those upvotes have monetary value in STEEM tokens. After a seven-day period, the accumulated value is automatically transferred to your wallet. You can then withdraw these tokens to cryptocurrency exchanges or staking them to power up your account.

Is my content truly mine on cur8.fun?

Absolutely. You retain full copyright and ownership of all content you publish. The blockchain provides immutable proof of your authorship, offering stronger intellectual property protection than traditional platforms. Your content can never be arbitrarily removed by the platform.

What types of content can I share?

You can share virtually any type of creative content: articles, blog posts, photography, artwork, tutorials, reviews, and more. Our advanced editor supports rich media embedding and formatting to make your content shine.

How do I get started with cur8.fun?

Getting started is simple! Click the "Create Your Account" button at the top of this page. You'll follow a quick signup process to choose a username (REMEMBER TO STORE A COPY OF YOUR ACCOUNT KEYS). Once registered, you can login using your POSTING key and customize your profile, follow creators who interest you, and begin publishing your own content immediately.

Are there any fees to use cur8.fun?

No! Creating an account and using the platform is completely free. There are no hidden fees, subscription costs, or transaction fees for basic activities. The STEEM blockchain allows frictionless transactions without the gas fees common on other blockchains.
